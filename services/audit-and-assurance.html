Audit and Assurance Services

Venture Partners CPA Limited provides statutory audit and assurance services for Hong Kong companies, assisting clients in meeting their reporting obligations under the Hong Kong Companies Ordinance and applicable financial reporting framework.

We work with directors, business owners, management teams and finance personnel to carry out audit engagements in a practical and organised manner, with clear communication throughout the audit process.

How We Assist

We assist clients with annual statutory audits, review of financial statements, audit planning, audit schedules coordination, discussion of accounting treatments, audit adjustments, tax related audit matters and completion of audit reporting procedures.

For Hong Kong private companies, the annual audit process is closely connected with profits tax filing, accounting records, board approval of financial statements and corporate compliance. Our audit approach therefore considers not only the financial statements, but also the practical compliance timeline faced by the client.

Where clients are part of an overseas group, we can also assist with local audit coordination, reporting package support and communication with group finance teams or overseas auditors where appropriate.

Common Situations

  • Hong Kong private companies requiring annual statutory audit
  • Companies preparing profits tax return filing
  • Owner managed businesses requiring properly audited financial statements
  • Overseas owned Hong Kong companies requiring local audit support
  • Trading, service and investment holding companies
  • Companies with accounting estimates, provisions or tax related audit matters
  • Businesses requiring audit schedules and year end closing support

Our Approach

We focus on proper planning, practical communication and timely follow up. A well managed audit should not be a last minute document chasing exercise. It should help management identify reporting issues, documentation gaps, internal process observations and tax related matters early enough for proper handling.

Our objective is to complete the audit with professional care while helping clients understand the key issues affecting their financial reporting and compliance position.
