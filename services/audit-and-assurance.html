Practice Area 01服務範疇 01

Statutory audit, handled end to end.

法定審計，由頭至尾全程跟進。

Statutory audit and assurance services for Hong Kong companies — financial statement audit, audit schedule preparation, reporting coordination and the tax-related matters that always sit alongside.

為香港公司提供法定審計及鑑證服務 — 包括財務報表審計、審計工作底稿準備、報告協調，以及與審計同步的稅務相關事項。

Our work

工作範圍

Venture Partners CPA Limited carries out statutory audit and assurance engagements for Hong Kong companies, assisting clients in meeting their reporting obligations under the Hong Kong Companies Ordinance and the applicable financial reporting framework.

本所為香港公司提供法定審計及鑑證服務，協助客戶履行《香港公司條例》及適用財務報告準則下的申報責任。

We work with directors, business owners, management teams and finance personnel to plan and execute audits in a properly documented manner — with written timelines, clear scoping and partner review of the working papers and the report itself.

我們與董事、企業擁有人、管理團隊及財務同事緊密合作，依書面時間表規劃及執行審計工作，並由合夥人親自審閱底稿及最終報告。

How we assist

我們如何協助

Our work covers annual statutory audits, review of financial statements, audit planning, working paper coordination, discussion of accounting treatments, audit adjustments, tax-related audit matters and completion of audit reporting procedures.

我們的工作涵蓋周年法定審計、財務報表審閱、審計計劃、工作底稿協調、會計處理討論、審計調整、稅務相關審計事項，以及完成審計報告程序。

For Hong Kong private companies, the audit cycle runs alongside profits tax filing, board approvals and corporate compliance — and we treat it as one coordinated workstream rather than three separate ones.

對於香港私人公司而言，審計周期與利得稅申報、董事會審批及企業合規同步進行 — 我們將之視為一條協調的工作流，而非三個獨立的工作。

Where clients are part of an overseas group, we can coordinate local audit work with group reporting packages and overseas auditors, with proper documentation of the bridging entries where they arise.

若客戶為海外集團之一員，我們可協調本地審計工作與集團報告，並與海外核數師溝通，妥善處理橋接分錄。

  • Companies with accounting estimates, provisions or tax-related audit matters涉及會計估算、撥備或稅務相關審計事項之公司
  • Businesses requiring audit schedules and year-end closing support需要審計底稿及年結支援之企業

Our approach

服務方針

A well-managed audit should not be a last-minute document chase. With proper planning and written follow-up, it surfaces reporting issues, documentation gaps and tax positions early — when there is still time to handle them properly.

管理得宜的審計，不應是年結前最後一刻的文件追逐戰。妥善的計劃及書面跟進，能讓財務報告議題、文件缺漏及稅務立場提早浮現 — 趁仍有時間妥善處理。

Our objective is to complete the audit with professional care, while helping clients understand the issues that actually affect their financial reporting and compliance position.

我們的目標，是以專業審慎之態度完成審計，並協助客戶了解真正影響其財務報告及合規狀況的議題。
