Support for SFC Licensed Corporations and Regulated Entities

Venture Partners CPA Limited assists SFC licensed corporations and regulated businesses with audit, accounting and financial reporting support.

Regulated entities require careful coordination between accounting records, audit procedures, management accounts, tax filing, financial resources considerations and regulatory reporting timelines. Our role is to provide practical professional support with attention to documentation, deadlines and compliance related financial information.

How We Assist

We assist with statutory audit coordination, financial statement preparation support, management accounts review, audit schedules, tax filing coordination, financial reporting matters, audit adjustments and documentation support for licensed corporations and regulated entities.

Our support may also cover accounting matters relevant to financial resources reporting, including provisions, accruals, tax balances, receivables, payables, management fee income, operating expenses and other balances affecting financial reporting or management accounts.

We can assist management and finance teams in preparing information required for audit, coordinating with compliance personnel, reviewing supporting schedules and identifying reporting matters that may require timely follow up.

Common Situations

  • SFC licensed corporations
  • Asset management companies
  • Advisory businesses
  • Fund managers
  • Regulated businesses requiring audit and accounting support
  • Licensed corporations preparing annual audited financial statements
  • Entities requiring support on management accounts and audit schedules
  • Businesses requiring coordination between finance, audit and compliance teams

Practical Areas of Support

  • Annual statutory audit coordination
  • Preparation or review of audit schedules
  • Management accounts support
  • Financial statement preparation support
  • Tax computation and profits tax filing coordination
  • Review of audit adjustments and related accounting impact
  • Provision for taxation and accruals review
  • Financial resources related accounting support
  • SFC audit questionnaire support from an accounting and audit coordination perspective
  • Coordination with compliance team or external advisers where appropriate

Our Approach

We understand that regulated businesses require a higher level of discipline in financial reporting and documentation. Small accounting issues can become larger problems when they affect management accounts, financial resources, audit reporting or regulatory submissions.

Our approach is practical, careful and compliance focused. We aim to help clients maintain accounting information that is properly organised, audit ready and suitable for management and compliance review.
