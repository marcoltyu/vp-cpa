Discipline for the regulated side of finance.

為金融業受規管一方而設的紀律。

Audit, accounting and FRR-related financial reporting support for SFC licensed corporations, asset managers and regulated entities — work that requires a higher level of discipline in documentation and review.

為證監會持牌法團、資產管理公司及受規管實體提供審計、會計及財政資源規則相關之財務報告支援 — 此類工作對文件及覆核要求更高。

Our work

工作範圍

We assist SFC licensed corporations and regulated businesses with audit, accounting and financial reporting support.

我們為證監會持牌法團及受規管業務提供審計、會計及財務報告支援。

Regulated entities require careful coordination between accounting records, audit procedures, management accounts, tax filing, financial resources considerations and regulatory reporting timelines. Our role is to provide practical professional support with attention to documentation, deadlines and compliance-related financial information.

受規管實體的會計記錄、審計程序、管理賬目、稅務申報、財政資源考量及規管報告時間表之間，需要小心協調。我們的角色，是提供實務的專業支援，並對文件、期限及合規相關財務資料保持關注。

How we assist

我們如何協助

We assist with statutory audit coordination, financial statement preparation support, management accounts review, audit schedules, tax filing coordination, financial reporting matters, audit adjustments and documentation support for licensed corporations and regulated entities.

我們協助法定審計協調、財務報表編製支援、管理賬目審閱、審計底稿、稅務申報協調、財務報告事項、審計調整，以及為持牌法團及受規管實體提供文件支援。

Our support extends to accounting matters relevant to financial resources reporting — provisions, accruals, tax balances, receivables, payables, management fee income, operating expenses and other balances affecting financial reporting or management accounts.

我們的支援延伸至與財政資源報告相關的會計事項 — 撥備、應計、稅項結餘、應收及應付帳項、管理費收入、營運開支，以及其他影響財務報告或管理賬目之結餘。

We assist management and finance teams in preparing information required for audit, coordinating with compliance personnel, reviewing supporting schedules and identifying reporting matters that require timely follow-up.

我們協助管理層及財務團隊準備審計所需資料，與合規同事協調，審閱支援底稿，並識別需要及時跟進之報告事項。

Our approach

服務方針

Regulated businesses require a higher level of discipline in financial reporting and documentation. Small accounting issues can become larger problems quickly when they affect management accounts, financial resources, audit reporting or regulatory submissions.

受規管業務對財務報告及文件的紀律要求更高。微小的會計問題，一旦影響管理賬目、財政資源、審計報告或監管申報，便會迅速放大。

Our approach is practical, careful and compliance-focused — accounting information that is properly organised, audit-ready and suitable for management and compliance review.

我們的方針務實、審慎，並以合規為核心 — 維持條理分明、可隨時供審計及合規覆核所用的會計資料。
