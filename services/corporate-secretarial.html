Corporate Secretarial Services

Venture Partners CPA Limited provides corporate secretarial support for Hong Kong companies, assisting clients in maintaining statutory records and meeting company filing requirements.

Proper company maintenance is important for corporate governance, banking, audit, tax filing, shareholder arrangements and business transactions. Missed filings or inconsistent records can create avoidable complications when a company later needs banking support, due diligence, restructuring or ownership changes.

How We Assist

We assist with annual return filings, business registration renewal coordination, maintenance of statutory registers, changes in directors, changes in shareholders, changes in registered office, share transfers, allotment of shares, company particulars updates and other company maintenance matters.

We also assist clients in understanding filing deadlines, required documents, board or shareholder approvals and practical steps for common corporate changes.

Where company records need to be aligned for audit, banking or transaction purposes, we can assist in reviewing the relevant statutory information and identifying inconsistencies requiring follow up.

Common Situations

  • Hong Kong private companies
  • Start ups and newly incorporated companies
  • Owner managed businesses
  • Overseas owned Hong Kong companies
  • Investment holding companies
  • Companies with changes in directors or shareholders
  • Companies undergoing share transfers or allotments
  • Businesses requiring annual company maintenance support

Our Approach

We provide organised company secretarial support with attention to statutory deadlines, proper documentation and consistency of corporate records.

Corporate secretarial work may look routine, but incomplete filings and inconsistent statutory records can create real problems later. Our role is to help clients keep their corporate records properly maintained and ready for audit, tax, banking and business purposes.
