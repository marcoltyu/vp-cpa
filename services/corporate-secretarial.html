Practice Area 04服務範疇 04

Statutory records, kept in order.

法定登記冊，井然有序。

Annual returns, statutory registers, share transfers and director or shareholder changes — kept consistent, on schedule and ready for audit, banking or transaction review.

周年申報、法定登記冊、股權轉讓、董事及股東變更 — 保持一致、依期辦理，並可隨時供審計、銀行或交易盡職審查所用。

Our work

工作範圍

We provide corporate secretarial support for Hong Kong companies — maintaining statutory records and meeting company filing requirements with proper documentation.

我們為香港公司提供公司秘書支援 — 維持法定登記冊，並依規完成公司申報，配以妥善文件記錄。

Routine company maintenance has a way of becoming important. Missed filings or inconsistent statutory records create avoidable complications later, when a company needs banking support, due diligence, restructuring or ownership changes.

看似例行的公司維護，往往在關鍵時刻才顯重要。漏報或登記冊不一致，會在公司日後尋求銀行支援、盡職審查、重組或股權變更時，造成不必要的麻煩。

How we assist

我們如何協助

We handle annual return filings, business registration renewal coordination, maintenance of statutory registers, changes in directors, changes in shareholders, changes in registered office, share transfers, allotment of shares, company particulars updates and other company maintenance matters.

我們處理周年申報表呈交、商業登記續期協調、法定登記冊維護、董事變更、股東變更、註冊辦事處變更、股權轉讓、新股配發、公司資料更新，以及其他公司維護事宜。

We help clients track filing deadlines, required documents, board or shareholder approvals and the practical steps for common corporate changes.

我們協助客戶追蹤申報期限、所需文件、董事會或股東批准，以及常見公司變更之實務步驟。

Where company records need to align for audit, banking or transaction purposes, we review the relevant statutory information and identify inconsistencies that require follow-up.

若公司記錄需與審計、銀行或交易用途相符，我們會審視相關法定資料，識別需要跟進的不一致之處。

Our approach

服務方針

Corporate secretarial work may look routine, but incomplete filings and inconsistent statutory records create real problems later — usually at the worst possible moment.

公司秘書工作看似例行，但漏報及登記冊不一致，往往在最不適合的時刻釀成大問題。

Our role is to keep corporate records properly maintained and ready for audit, tax, banking and business purposes — quietly, on time and on paper.

我們的角色，是讓公司記錄妥善維持，並能隨時供審計、稅務、銀行及業務用途所需 — 靜靜地、準時、留有書面記錄。
