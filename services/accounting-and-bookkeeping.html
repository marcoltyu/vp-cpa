Practice Area 03服務範疇 03

Accounting records built for audit and decision-making.

為審計與決策而建的會計記錄。

Management accounts, bank reconciliations, year-end closing and audit-ready schedules — accounting records that work as a control tool, not a storage box for invoices.

管理賬目、銀行對賬、年結及達致審計水平之底稿 — 我們相信會計記錄應是管理工具，而非發票倉庫。

Our work

工作範圍

We provide accounting and bookkeeping support to businesses that require proper financial records, reliable management accounts and audit-ready supporting schedules.

我們為企業提供會計及簿記支援，協助維持完善的財務記錄、可靠的管理賬目，以及達致審計水平之支援底稿。

Proper accounting records sit behind everything — statutory audit, profits tax filing, management reporting, banking, financing and business planning. Most weak accounting issues only become visible when an audit or tax deadline is closing in, which is rarely the cheapest time to fix them.

妥善的會計記錄，是法定審計、利得稅申報、管理報告、銀行往來、融資及業務規劃的根本。會計上的弱點，往往要到審計或報稅死線臨近時才浮現 — 而那絕非處理問題的最佳時機。

How we assist

我們如何協助

Our work covers bookkeeping, maintenance of accounting records, bank reconciliations, preparation of management accounts, year-end closing, accounting schedules, audit preparation, tax filing coordination and review of financial information.

我們的工作涵蓋簿記、會計記錄維護、銀行對賬、管理賬目編製、年結、會計底稿、審計準備、稅務申報協調，以及財務資料審閱。

We help structure the accounting in a way that supports later steps — classification of income and expenses, supporting schedules for receivables, payables, fixed assets, accruals, prepayments, related-party balances and tax-related accounts.

我們協助建立有結構的會計系統 — 收入及開支分類、應收及應付帳項、固定資產、應計、預付、關連方結餘及稅務相關帳項等支援底稿。

For clients without an in-house finance team, we can act as the practical extension of one — keeping the records organised and aligned with audit and tax compliance needs.

對於沒有專屬財務團隊的客戶，我們可擔任實務上的延伸 — 確保記錄井然有序，並與審計及稅務合規需要同步。

Common situations

常見情況

  • Start-ups and growing businesses初創及成長中之業務
  • Owner-managed companies without an in-house finance team未設立內部財務團隊之家族經營公司
  • Companies preparing for audit正準備接受審計之公司
  • Overseas-owned Hong Kong entities requiring local accounting support需要本地會計支援之海外股東持有香港公司
  • Businesses requiring monthly or quarterly management accounts需要每月或每季管理賬目之企業
  • Companies needing year-end closing and audit schedule preparation需要年結及審計底稿準備之公司
  • Clients seeking cleaner records for tax filing and management review尋求更清晰記錄以便報稅及管理層審視之客戶

Our approach

服務方針

Good accounting records do more than satisfy a statutory requirement. They reduce audit queries, improve tax filing quality and let management actually understand the business — which is, after all, what the numbers are for.

良好的會計記錄，不只滿足法定要求 — 它能減少審計查詢、提升報稅質素，並讓管理層真正理解業務。畢竟，數字的存在，本就為此。

Our aim is to set up accounting that works as a control tool, not just a place to file invoices.

我們的目標，是把會計建立成一個管理工具，而非一個堆放發票的儲物櫃。
