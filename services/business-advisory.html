Practice Area 05服務範疇 05

Practical advice, grounded in the actual numbers.

切實可行的建議，立足於真實數字之上。

Practical accounting, tax and corporate advice on operations, restructuring, expansion, financial reporting and commercial transactions — the kind that can be acted on, not filed away.

就營運、重組、擴展、財務報告及商業交易提供實務的會計、稅務及企業意見 — 是可付諸行動的建議，而非束之高閣的文件。

Our work

工作範圍

We provide practical business advisory support on the accounting, tax and corporate matters that arise from business operations, restructuring, expansion and commercial transactions.

我們就營運、重組、擴展及商業交易所引起之會計、稅務及企業事項，提供實務顧問支援。

Our advisory work is grounded in professional knowledge and practical business understanding. We focus on issues that affect financial reporting, tax exposure, corporate compliance, internal control, business planning and management decisions.

我們的顧問工作，紮根於專業知識及商業理解，聚焦於影響財務報告、稅務負擔、企業合規、內部控制、業務規劃及管理決策的議題。

How we assist

我們如何協助

We assist with accounting and tax considerations for business decisions, review of financial information, business structure discussion, transaction support, internal control observations, financial reporting issues and coordination with other professional advisers.

我們協助處理業務決策中的會計及稅務考量、財務資料審閱、業務架構討論、交易支援、內部控制觀察、財務報告事項，並可協調其他專業顧問之配合。

We also support management in identifying practical issues arising from business growth, shareholder changes, new business models, cross-border operations, financing needs, restructuring and commercial arrangements.

我們亦支援管理層識別業務擴展、股東變動、新業務模式、跨境營運、融資需要、重組及商業安排所引起的實務議題。

Our role may include reviewing management accounts, discussing the accounting or tax impact of proposed transactions, identifying documentation needs, and helping management understand the practical implications before decisions are finalised.

我們的角色或包括審閱管理賬目、討論擬定交易的會計或稅務影響、釐清所需文件，並協助管理層在決策落實前理解實際影響。

Common situations

常見情況

  • Business owners considering expansion or restructuring考慮擴展或重組之企業擁有人
  • Companies reviewing business performance and financial information正檢視業績及財務資料之公司
  • Clients planning new business arrangements規劃新業務安排之客戶
  • Businesses with shareholder or ownership changes面對股東或股權變更之業務
  • Companies considering financing or investment transactions考慮融資或投資交易之公司
  • Cross-border businesses requiring Hong Kong accounting or tax input需要香港會計或稅務意見之跨境業務
  • Management teams seeking practical advice on reporting, tax or compliance implications尋求報告、稅務或合規意見之管理團隊

Our approach

服務方針

We do not treat advisory work as abstract theory. Our focus is to provide advice that is clear, commercially relevant and capable of being implemented.

我們不會把顧問工作視為抽象理論。我們的目標，是提供清晰、具商業相關性，並可實際執行的意見。

We work with clients to understand the facts, assess the implications and identify sensible next steps. Where a matter involves legal, valuation, licensing or other specialist considerations, we coordinate with the relevant advisers while focusing on the accounting, tax and corporate aspects.

我們與客戶一同了解事實、評估影響，並釐清合理的下一步。若事項涉及法律、估值、牌照或其他專業範疇，我們會與相關顧問協調，並專注於會計、稅務及企業層面之意見。
