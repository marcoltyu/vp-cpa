Hong Kong tax compliance, properly supported.

香港稅務合規，妥善支援。

Profits tax filing, tax computations, IRD correspondence, objections, holdover applications and assessment review — handled with proper documentation and a clear written trail.

利得稅申報、稅項計算、稅局函件往來、反對申請、緩繳申請及評稅檢視 — 均配以妥善文件及清晰書面紀錄處理。

Our work

工作範圍

We provide Hong Kong tax compliance and practical tax advisory support to companies and business owners — covering profits tax filings, tax computations, responses to the Inland Revenue Department and the tax matters that arise from day-to-day business operations.

我們為公司及企業擁有人提供香港稅務合規及實務稅務顧問支援，涵蓋利得稅申報、稅項計算、稅務局函件回覆，以及日常營運所衍生的稅務事項。

Tax compliance in Hong Kong is closely connected with accounting treatments, audited financial statements, tax assessments, provisional tax and IRD correspondence. We help clients manage these matters in a structured and properly supported manner.

香港的稅務合規與會計處理、審計後財務報表、稅務評估、暫繳稅及稅局信件息息相關。我們協助客戶以有系統、有文件支援的方式管理。

How we assist

我們如何協助

We assist with preparation of profits tax computations, completion and filing of profits tax returns, review of notices of assessment, handling of IRD enquiries, objection support, holdover applications, estimated assessment matters, provisional tax review and tax compliance advice.

我們協助準備利得稅計算、填寫及提交利得稅報稅表、審閱評稅通知書、處理稅局查詢、反對申請支援、緩繳申請、估稅事項、暫繳稅檢視，以及稅務合規意見。

We also consider the tax implications of accounting treatments, deductible expenses, offshore or cross-border arrangements, related-party transactions, directors' remuneration, management charges and other commercial matters where relevant.

我們亦會視乎情況，分析會計處理、可扣除開支、離岸或跨境安排、關連方交易、董事薪酬、管理費等商業事項的稅務影響。

Where a client receives an IRD enquiry or estimated assessment, we review the background, prepare written explanations and supporting documents, and communicate with the IRD in a professional manner.

若客戶收到稅局查詢或估稅通知，我們會審視背景、準備書面解釋及支援文件，並以專業方式與稅局溝通。

  • Companies needing tax computations aligned with audited financial statements需要與審計報表一致之稅項計算之公司

Our approach

服務方針

Tax matters are rarely solved by quoting rules alone. The commercial facts, accounting records, supporting documents and audit position often matter just as much.

稅務問題鮮少可單憑條文解答 — 商業事實、會計記錄、支援文件及審計立場，往往同樣重要。

Our approach is to understand the facts first, assess the tax position, identify documentation requirements and assist clients in responding properly and efficiently — with written follow-up at each step.

我們的方針，是先了解事實，繼而評估稅務立場、釐清所需文件，並協助客戶妥善有效地回應 — 每一步均以書面跟進。
