Taxation Services

Venture Partners CPA Limited provides Hong Kong tax compliance and practical tax advisory support to companies and business owners. We assist clients with profits tax filings, tax computations, responses to the Inland Revenue Department and tax matters arising from business operations.

Tax compliance in Hong Kong is often closely connected with accounting treatments, audited financial statements, tax assessments, provisional tax and IRD correspondence. We help clients manage these matters in a structured and properly supported manner.

How We Assist

We assist with preparation of profits tax computations, completion and filing of profits tax returns, review of notices of assessment, handling of IRD enquiries, objection support, holdover applications, estimated assessment matters, provisional tax review and tax compliance advice.

We also assist clients in considering the tax implications of accounting treatments, deductible expenses, offshore or cross border arrangements, related party transactions, directors’ remuneration, management charges and other commercial matters where relevant.

Where a client receives an IRD enquiry or estimated assessment, we can assist in reviewing the background, preparing explanations, gathering supporting documents and communicating with the IRD in a professional manner.

Common Situations

  • Companies preparing annual profits tax filings
  • Businesses receiving IRD enquiries or estimated assessments
  • Clients considering objection or holdover applications
  • Companies with provisional tax issues
  • Businesses with tax losses or significant year on year changes
  • Overseas owned Hong Kong companies requiring local tax compliance
  • Owner managed businesses requiring practical tax filing support
  • Companies needing tax computations aligned with audited financial statements

Our Approach

We aim to provide clear, practical and properly supported tax advice. Tax matters are rarely solved by quoting rules alone. The commercial facts, accounting records, supporting documents and audit position often matter just as much.

Our approach is to understand the facts, assess the tax position, identify documentation requirements and assist clients in responding properly and efficiently.
