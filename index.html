01 · Our position01 · 我們的定位

Audit, tax and accounting matters are not annual obligations to be filed and forgotten. They shape tax exposure, financing, governance and the long-term direction of a business.

審計、稅務及會計從不只是年度申報；它們深刻影響企業的稅務負擔、融資能力、企業管治，以至長遠發展。

We work with directors, business owners and management teams on the practical issues that sit behind the financial statements — accounting treatments, IRD positions, related-party arrangements, board approvals, group reporting and the tax timeline that runs alongside every audit.

我們與董事、企業擁有人及管理團隊緊密合作，處理財務報表背後的實際問題 — 會計處理、稅務局立場、關連方安排、董事會審批、集團報告，以及與每項審計同步推進的稅務時間表。

Our role is to keep statutory compliance on schedule while ensuring that what management sees in the numbers is accurate, defensible and actionable — whether that means an IRD enquiry response, an audit adjustment discussion, a share-transfer review, or a quiet word about something that ought to be tidied up before the next year-end.

我們的工作，是確保法定合規如期完成，同時讓管理層所見的數字準確、有據可查、可以付諸行動 — 不論是稅務局查詢的回覆、審計調整的討論、股權轉讓的審視，又或是下一個年結前應該整理好的細節提醒。

Our Approach服務方針

Four working principles. Held to on every engagement.

四項工作原則，每項委聘皆然。

A professional file is not graded on intention. It is graded on what was done, how it was documented and what the client can rely on at year-end. We work to that standard.

一份專業檔案不會以「用心」評分，而是以「做了甚麼、如何記錄、客戶可依賴何物」來評分。我們以此為標準。

i

Partner-led, not partner-introduced.

合夥人主理，並非僅引薦

A partner reviews the file, signs the report and is reachable through the engagement — not only at the kick-off meeting.

合夥人親自審閱檔案、簽署報告，並於委聘期間隨時可聯絡 — 而非只於開展會議時露面。

ii

Compliance handled on schedule.

合規工作如期完成

Statutory audit, profits tax and corporate filings tracked with proper deadlines, supporting documents and board approvals.

法定審計、利得稅及公司申報均按期限管理，配以妥善文件及董事會審批。

iii

Commercial issues read alongside the numbers.

業務議題與數字同步審視

Accounting treatments, related-party arrangements and tax positions are reviewed in the context of the actual business.

會計處理、關連方安排及稅務立場，皆以實際業務為背景作出檢視。

iv

Direct, written follow-up.

清晰書面跟進

Issues are recorded in writing, with what was done, what is outstanding and what the client should action before the next milestone.

所有事項以書面記錄：已處理、待跟進，以及客戶於下一節點前應採取的行動，一目了然。
