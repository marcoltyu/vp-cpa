Recommended image files before launch:

1. vpcpa-logo.png
   Header logo, transparent background.

2. favicon.png
   32 x 32 or 64 x 64 PNG, preferably using the VP mark.

3. apple-touch-icon.png
   180 x 180 PNG, preferably using the VP mark.

4. og-image.jpg
   1200 x 630 JPG for WhatsApp, LinkedIn and social sharing preview.
