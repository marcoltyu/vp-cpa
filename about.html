About VPCPA

Practical professional support, led by experienced practitioners.

Venture Partners CPA Limited is a certified public accountants practice providing audit, tax, accounting, corporate secretarial and business advisory services to clients operating in and through Hong Kong.

Our Firm

Professional advice with practical business understanding.

We work with private companies, owner managed businesses, start ups, overseas owned Hong Kong entities, professional firms and regulated businesses.

Our role is to help clients manage statutory audit, tax filing, accounting and corporate compliance matters in a properly organised manner, while considering the commercial issues behind the numbers.

We believe professional services should be technically sound, clearly communicated and practical enough for management to act on.
