The Practice事務所簡介

A Hong Kong practice, built around partner-level attention.

立足香港，合夥人主理的會計師事務所。

Venture Partners CPA Limited is a four-partner Hong Kong practice supporting private companies, owner-managed groups, overseas-owned Hong Kong entities and SFC licensed corporations. We have been doing this — quietly and consistently — since 2004.

Venture Partners CPA Limited 為四位合夥人主理的香港會計師事務所，服務私人企業、家族經營集團、海外股東持有公司，以及證監會持牌法團。我們自2004年起，一直默默而穩健地耕耘。

Our Story事務所故事

Founded in 2004. Built one engagement at a time.

2004年成立，逐宗委聘累積而成。

VPCPA was founded by experienced practitioners who believed Hong Kong's smaller and mid-sized businesses deserved the same standard of audit and tax discipline as larger groups, delivered by partners who would actually pick up the phone.

VPCPA 由資深執業會計師創辦，創辦人深信香港的中小型企業，理應與大型集團一樣，享有同等水平的審計及稅務專業 — 而且，是由真的會接電話的合夥人主理。

Two decades later, we serve more than three hundred client engagements each year, from a four-partner practice supported by a team of thirty-plus professionals across audit, tax, accounting and corporate secretarial work. We have grown deliberately, and only as quickly as we could grow well.

二十多年過去，我們每年服務超過三百項客戶委聘，由四位合夥人領導逾三十位審計、稅務、會計及公司秘書專業同事的團隊。我們刻意穩步成長 — 只擴張到能保持服務質素的限度為止。

How We Work工作方式

Clear, organised and commercially relevant.

清晰、有條理、兼具商業視角。

Engagements run on documented timelines, written follow-up and proper supporting papers. We treat our deliverables as something a third party — a regulator, a banker, a future buyer — should be able to read and understand.

所有委聘均依書面時間表及跟進記錄推進，配以妥善支援文件。我們的交付成果，要能讓第三方（監管機構、銀行、未來投資者）翻閱即明白。

01

Scoping conversation

範圍商討

A partner discusses the engagement, the timeline, the supporting documents required and any matters likely to need attention.

合夥人與客戶商討委聘範圍、時間表、所需文件，以及可能需要特別關注的事項。

02

Planning & preparation

計劃及準備

A working timetable is set out in writing, with milestones, document lists and the people responsible on each side.

以書面訂立工作時間表，列明關鍵節點、文件清單，以及雙方各自負責的同事。

03

Fieldwork & review

實地工作及審閱

Audit, tax or accounting work is carried out by our team, with partner review and written communication of any issues identified.

由團隊執行審計、稅務或會計工作，並由合夥人審閱；任何發現事項均以書面與客戶溝通。

04

Sign-off & close-out

簽署及完成

Reports are signed by the responsible partner. A written close-out note records what was done and what should be tidied up before next year.

報告由負責合夥人簽署；以書面完成備忘記錄已處理事項，以及下一年度前應整理的細節。
